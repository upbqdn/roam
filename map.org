<<.df-2>> *[[.df-2][Definition 2]].* Let $A$ and $B$ be sets. A *map* is a set
  $\varphi \subseteq$ [[id:cartesian-product][$A \times B$]] such that for each $a \in A$
  there is exactly one $b \in B$ with $\left( a, b \right) \in \varphi$.
